  • February 13, 2017 Toptal!

    • Its been a long time since I've touched this site and I plan a massive refactor as I get reacquainted with a more modern framework, but I'm so excited to be applying for Toptal, a hiring board for remote developers! Recently my step father has gotten very ill, which is why I decided to take time off, do some caretaking, and really reevaluate what makes me happy. Freelancing is something I've always wanted to do as an iOS/Android developer but never had the courage to embrace. I'm excited to finally take the big leap!
    Working as a freelance mobile developer in the Toptal mobile development group will allow me to take care of my family while simultaneously being able to do what I love: code! As someone who has been part of the startup environment for about three years, I know what it's like to wear many different hats- to design and build software architecture from scratch and feel the pressure of making a good first impression on apps. End users have an increasingly short attention span when it comes to new apps which means its important to get the minimal viable product right the first time. Freelancing will give me an awesome opportunity to think along these lines and to build things from a bird's eye perspective because I get to work directly with the client.
